SIZE GUIDE

Choose the right sunglasses or eyeglasses size by simply using your credit card and a mirror.

For the great majority of people, more then 90% of our customers, Standard Size is the correct one.
It’s very likely it’s going to be the correct size for you too.

A rule of thumb to determine the correct size for you is the following.

FIND YOUR FIT:

1

Take a credit card

This is the approximate width of a Standard Size lens.

2

Stand in front of the mirror

In front of a mirror or a webcam, place one edge of the credit card at the center of your nose. Where does the other edge end?

3

What is your Ray-Ban size?

  • A. If the edge of the credit card ends at the end of the eye your fit should be Standard Size
  • B. If it extends well beyond the end of the eye you should get a Small Size.
  • C. If instead the credit card does not reach the end of the eye then go for a Large Size.
